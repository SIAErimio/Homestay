About us

Welcome to our house of Bertelè.

Our cozy homestay is located in Hanoi — the vibrant capital of Vietnam.
We warmly welcome travelers from near and far to enjoy a peaceful and stylish stay in our home.

Our house features:

A soundproof system for total relaxation.
Modern design and fully equipped interiors.
A comfortable, welcoming atmosphere for all guests.
A fully stocked kitchen with everything you need to cook your favorite meals.
Whether you're visiting for work or leisure, we're here to make your stay unforgettable.

Come stay with us and feel at home in our house!
